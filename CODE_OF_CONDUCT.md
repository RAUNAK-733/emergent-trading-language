# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participants are expected to communicate respectfully, welcome constructive
criticism, and focus discussion on evidence and the work itself.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory
language, deliberate disruption, and publishing another person's private
information.

## Research Conduct

Contributors must not fabricate results, hide failed controls, misrepresent
preliminary findings, or submit generated output as independently verified
evidence.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove inappropriate content and may restrict
participation when necessary. Report conduct concerns privately to the
maintainer through GitHub.
